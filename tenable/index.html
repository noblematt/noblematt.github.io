Tenable Quiz Game

Welcome to the Tenable quiz game, where you can create a question with a list of ten answers, and test your friends' knowledge. To create a question, simply fill in the form below with the question and ten answers. You can also optionally add clues or contexts to the answers.

For example, you might have a question such as "ten countries by their capital city", and then the clue field for each answer would be the capital city. Or you might have a question where the answers are the first ten films in a given category, ordered by release date. To help out your players, you can add in the year they were released in the context field, so that this is displayed along with the answers.

At any time, you can take a copy of the URL to save the details you have entered. The URL string contains an encoding of everything entered so far. Once you are happy with the question, you can enter the list of players and click Start Game.

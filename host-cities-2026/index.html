2026 World Cup Host Cities Analysis

Travel booking is going to be extremely challenging for fans who wish to follow their teams to all matches at the 2026 FIFA World Cup. Only three groups have got confirmed host cities for all of their matches. In the other nine groups, there are two cities for each matchday and the allocation of matches to cities will not be confirmed until after the draw.

A consequence of this is that there will now be 18 different scenarios a team can find themselves in. In nine of those scenarios, they would be in a group with a host nation and would know exactly where they will be playing on each matchday. In the other nine, they would have a degree of uncertainty.

To assist with planning and booking travel, I have created a map for each of the 18 possible scenarios, showing the cities a team could play in and the order in which they would play in them. I will now create a page which adds further details to help in the travel-booking process.
